Current SY Divisions

Cast

- Members that will reenact on set
- Workshops consist of basic theatre tips, voice projection, etc.
- Bring life by reenacting the given material
- Headed by Anton Dasalla and Poji Mercado

Crew

- Members in charge of all-around management of the set
- Consists usually of creative staff, directors, scriptwriters, and stage managers
- Also handles set design, lighting, and props the actors will use
- Directs and envisions how the given material will be reenacted
- Handles all-around logistics by giving the set design life (through lighting, etc.)
- Headed by Laia Rabaya and Vien Castillo
Past SY Divisions

Writers

- Scriptwriters for the club; brings material to reenact

Actors

- Reenacts the given material; brings life to the writing

Creatives

- In charge of general set design and prop conception/making

Directors

- Directs the actors' according to their personal vision

Stage Managers

- Manages the stage during plays; fixes and moves props during the reenactments
